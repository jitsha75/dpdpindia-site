v2.1 March 2026 · Current
Five-Article Knowledge Base & Project Infrastructure
Expanded the eKYC KB from three to five articles with deep-dive compliance content; added the Project History page establishing version control discipline for the site.

Article 04 — Data Centre & CERT-IN Audit Checklist (ekyc-datacenter.html): Interactive 24-item audit readiness checklist covering physical security, logical hardening, ADV compliance, HSM operations, application security, and governance documentation under Compliance Checklist v2.0. Sidebar shows live audit readiness progress percentage as items are checked.

Sidebar includes an interactive fee estimator.

v0.1 Early 2026 · Pre-launch
Foundation & Research Scope
Project inception — definition of research scope, design system selection, and content architecture.
Structure
Research mandate defined: DPDPindia.in established as an independent policy and legal research initiative tracking the Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023, its draft rules, regulatory developments, and India's digital governance ecosystem. Focus on doctrinal legal analysis, comparative research, regulatory monitoring, and public commentary.
Structure
Content architecture: Decision to publish as flat-file HTML (no CMS dependency, fully offline-capable). Primary content areas: DPDP Act analysis, Digital Governance, AI Policy, Cybersecurity, Privacy Rights, Legal Analysis. Knowledge Base as a separate technical reference strand covering India's digital identity infrastructure where it intersects with DPDP.
Structure
Design direction: "Editorial Luxury Law Review" aesthetic selected — editorial serif typography (Playfair Display + Source Serif 4), Prussian blue with saffron accent, parchment backgrounds. Deliberately references traditional legal and academic publication aesthetics to signal rigour and independence.
