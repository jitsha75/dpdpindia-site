About this Knowledge Base
This knowledge base is published by DPDPindia.in as part of its independent research on India's digital identity infrastructure and its intersection with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023. The content is compiled from UIDAI's official API specifications, the Aadhaar Act 2016, Authentication Regulations 2021, and UIDAI Circulars 2025–26. It is updated as UIDAI publishes revised specifications and regulatory circulars. For production integrations, always verify against the latest official documents at uidai.gov.in.
