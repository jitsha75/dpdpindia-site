Five Articles on India's Cyber Law Framework

The Information Technology Act, 2000 is India's foundational statute for electronic governance, cybersecurity, and digital commerce. This KB traces its origins in international trade law, its major 2008 security amendment, its landmark judicial reinterpretation, and its continuing relevance in 2026.

IT Act Compliance for Research Platforms

01
Section 79 Safe Harbour
DPDPIndia.in claims safe harbour under Section 79 of the IT Act as an intermediary hosting educational research content. Immunity is conditioned on publishing Terms of Use, a Privacy Policy, and acting on court/government takedown orders within 36 hours.
02
IT Rules 2021 Compliance
This platform complies with the IT (Intermediary Guidelines) Rules 2021 — including grievance officer appointment, user content restrictions, and the due diligence framework under Rule 3. A Grievance Officer is reachable via the Contact page.
