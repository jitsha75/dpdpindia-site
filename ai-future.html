13 — Future of AI

The Future of Artificial Intelligence

A calibrated look at near-term certainties, medium-term probabilities, and long-term uncertainties in AI development — and their implications for law, regulation, and society in India.

Near Term · 2025–2027
AI Agents & Multimodal Integration
  • AI agents that autonomously plan and execute multi-step tasks (web research, code execution, email management) become mainstream enterprise tools
  • Multimodal AI (text + image + audio + video in a single model) becomes the dominant product paradigm
  • AI regulation implementation: EU AI Act prohibited uses enforced from August 2026; High Risk system requirements phased in
  • India's DPDP Rules finalisation triggers compliance requirements for AI data fiduciaries using personal data for AI
  • AI-powered legal research tools become standard in Indian law firms and courts — SUVAS/SUPACE expansion
  • AI deepfake detection becomes a contested legal evidence issue in Indian courts
Medium Term · 2027–2032
Reasoning AI & Sector Transformation
  • AI systems with genuine multi-step logical reasoning (not pattern matching) reshape knowledge work in law, medicine, and finance
  • India's IndiaAI Mission produces domestically competitive foundation models in Indian languages
  • AI-driven drug discovery accelerates: AlphaFold successors model protein interactions enabling faster clinical trials
  • Global AI governance treaties emerge — extending beyond the G7/OECD voluntary frameworks to binding international law
  • India's AI regulatory framework likely formalised — dedicated legislation or rules under the DPDP Act addressing automated decision-making
  • Significant displacement in legal, accounting, and back-office work — requiring proactive workforce transition policy
Long Term · 2032+ · High Uncertainty
AGI & Existential Questions
  • Artificial General Intelligence (AGI): AI systems matching or exceeding human cognitive performance across virtually all tasks — timelines remain deeply contested (expert estimates range from 5 to 50+ years)
  • Constitutional and fundamental rights implications of AI decision-making in governance — who has standing to challenge an AI's decision affecting them?
  • International AI treaties analogous to nuclear non-proliferation for the most capable AI systems
  • AI rights and personhood: legal and philosophical questions about AI moral status — currently theoretical but increasingly debated
  • Quantum computing + AI convergence: quantum hardware potentially accelerating AI training and enabling new AI paradigms
  • Note: Long-term AI forecasting is inherently speculative. Treat these as scenarios for strategic planning, not predictions.
Disclaimer on Future Projections

All future-oriented statements in this section are based on publicly available expert analysis, academic research, and policy reports as of mid-2025. AI development trajectories are inherently uncertain. These scenarios are provided for educational and strategic planning purposes only — not as predictions or investment advice. The AI field evolves rapidly; developments may materially differ from any projection within months.
