Golden Hour Rule — Financial Fraud
If money just left your account — act in the next 60 minutes
Cybercrime cells can freeze the destination account and reverse the transaction if reported within 1–2 hours. After 24 hours, money is usually withdrawn and recovery chances drop sharply. Do not waste time gathering evidence first — call immediately.

Universal Prevention

Four Rules That Prevent Most Frauds

🔴
Never Share OTP
No bank, UIDAI, TRAI, police, or government agency will ever call you asking for an OTP, PIN, or password. If anyone asks — it is fraud. Hang up immediately.
🟡
Request = Red Flag
Receiving money on UPI does not require entering your PIN. If someone tells you to "enter your PIN to accept money" — they are stealing from you.
🟢
Verify Before You Pay
Fake customer care numbers rank on Google. Always get contact numbers from the official app or the back of your card — never from a search result or forwarded message.
🔵
"Digital Arrest" Is Not Real
No court, CBI, ED, police, or Narcotics Bureau conducts arrests over video call. If you receive such a call — it is 100% fraud. Disconnect and call 1930.
